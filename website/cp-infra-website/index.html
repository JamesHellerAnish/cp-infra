Over 3 Decades of Infra-Excellence

C P Systems Pvt. Ltd., established in 1999, is not just a company — it is an institution built on a foundation of innovation, ingenuity, and purpose. With a firm belief in meaningful and timely project execution, CPS stands out for its creative approach, technical excellence, and unwavering commitment to quality.


After gaining experience and expertise, and profitably reaping the fruits of diversification, the portfolio of CPS has a variety in terms of both the activities undertaken and the clients served — Government & Semi Government Departments, Public Sector Undertakings, Turnkey Projects Operators and Promoters & Builders.

Our Expertise

Comprehensive Infrastructure Solutions

Encompassing diverse areas of construction pan India and beyond, CPS delivers end-to-end infrastructure solutions powered by innovation and commitment.

🏗️

Civil & Mechanical Works

Riverfront development, pedestrian bridges, civic center buildings, and pilgrimage accommodation — as executed at Badrinath Dham & Kedarnath Dham.

💧

Hydropower & River Dredging

Multi-phase river dredging, baffle wall construction across flowing rivers, and hydropower infrastructure — as executed at Tehri Dam Phase 1 & 2.

⛏️

Mining & Exploration

Granite quarrying, processing, and premium stone products — operating mines in Bhutan (85 acres) and Kekri, Rajasthan (1 Hectare).

🔩

Drilling & Grouting

Consolidation grouting with microfine cement, PU grouting, colloidal silica injection, and micro piling — as executed at Tehri HRT/TRT and VPHEP.

🧪

Epoxy & Repair Works

Epoxy mortar application, underwater epoxy treatment, seal coats, and surface repair — as executed at Tehri Intake Structures and HRT #3 & #4.

🏛️

Dam & Tunnel Rehabilitation

Gate groove restoration, spillway repair, tunnel lining, and structural strengthening — as executed at Hailang Dam VPHEP and Maneri Bhali Dam.
