Civil & Mechanical Construction

CPS delivers robust structures, efficient utilities, and integrated systems with unmatched durability and safety. Our expertise covers all aspects of civil construction — from foundation to finishing — including structural steel fabrication, reinforcement work, formwork, concreting, and MEP services.


Our team has delivered landmark projects at sacred pilgrimage sites under extreme terrain and weather conditions, as well as industrial complexes, treatment plants, and highway infrastructure across India.
