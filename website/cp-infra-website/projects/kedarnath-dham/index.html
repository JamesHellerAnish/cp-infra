Project Overview

Pilgrim Accommodation at Kedarnath Dham

Construction of Pilgrim Accommodation Block Q2Q at Shri Kedarnath Dham — one of India's most sacred pilgrimage sites. The project demanded construction in challenging Himalayan terrain with extreme altitude and weather conditions, making it a time-sensitive and technically complex endeavor.
