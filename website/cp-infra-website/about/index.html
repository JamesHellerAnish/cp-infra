Our Story

Built on Innovation, Ingenuity & Purpose

CPS is not just a company; it is an institution built on a foundation of innovation, ingenuity, and purpose. With a firm belief in meaningful and timely project execution, CPS stands out for its creative approach, technical excellence, and unwavering commitment to quality.


We take pride in completing complex infrastructure projects with a focus on innovation and client satisfaction. Our work is not only defined by what we build but by how we build it — with integrity, precision, and a forward-thinking mindset.


In addition to project execution, CPS is actively involved in the exploration and advancement of Civil and Structural Design, continually expanding its capabilities through strategic diversification. This progressive outlook has led to our successful foray into Turnkey Project Execution, reflecting our capacity to deliver end-to-end infrastructure solutions.

Recognition

Nationally Recognized Excellence

Our dedication to excellence has been recognized nationally with the "Udyog Ratan Award" and "Excellence Award" in 2000–2001, reinforcing our reputation as a reliable and visionary partner in the infrastructure sector.


At the core of CPS lies a team of highly skilled and experienced professionals. The strength of our workforce is reflected in its versatility of expertise, ranging from Civil, Anti-Corrosive, Mechanical, and Electrical Works Execution. Our team members bring with them a wealth of technical and practical knowledge, gained through years of service with leading infrastructure companies.


We attribute our success to the exceptional caliber of our personnel, many of whom have been associated with top-tier organizations, shaping the infrastructure development map of the nation.
